Chicken Noodle Salad

Description

Cool and refreshing, Chicken Noodle Salad is a perfect summer dish that's both flavorful and filling.
Shredded chicken mingles with cooked noodles, crisp vegetables, and a variety of herbs for a delightful combination of textures.
The dressing, often light and tangy, can be customized to your taste preferences, with options ranging from creamy to Asian-inspired with a peanut sauce base.
Whether served as a main course or a side dish, Chicken Noodle Salad is a crowd-pleaser that's sure to satisfy on a warm day.

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup seasoned rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha chile sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 cooked chicken breast
  • 1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup chopped roasted, salted peanuts
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh basil
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh mint
  • ⅓ cup chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Prepare the dressing: Whisk together your chosen dressing ingredients in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Cook the chicken and noodles according to package instructions. Shred the chicken and drain the noodles.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, noodles, and your favorite chopped vegetables.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat all ingredients evenly.
  5. (Optional) Garnish with chopped nuts, fresh herbs, or a sprinkle of sesame seeds before serving.
