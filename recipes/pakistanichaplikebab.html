Pakistani Chapli Kebab

Description

Pakistani Chapli Kebab is a flavorful and satisfying dish made with minced meat, lentils, and a blend of aromatic spices. Traditionally grilled over charcoal, Chapli Kebabs boast a crispy exterior and a juicy, tender interior. The unique blend of spices creates a symphony of flavors, making them a popular choice for street food and celebratory meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or lamb
  • ½ cup brown lentils, cooked and mashed
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • ½ teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to spice preference)
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil for greasing
  • (Optional) Chopped fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, mashed lentils, chopped onion, garlic, ginger, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  2. Using your hands, shape the mixture into thick patties. Aim for a slightly elongated oval shape.
  3. Heat a grill or grill pan over medium heat. Brush lightly with oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Gently place the Chapli Kebabs on the preheated grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. (Optional) Serve hot garnished with chopped fresh cilantro and enjoy with chutney or raita of your choice.
